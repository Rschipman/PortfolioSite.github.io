Portfolio Chatbot Assistant

This project explores how conversational AI can enhance a personal portfolio by providing instant, interactive answers to common recruiter and client questions.

Problem

Traditional portfolio sites require visitors to manually search through pages to understand a developer’s experience. Recruiters often want quick answers and may leave before finding relevant information.

Solution

I implemented an embedded AI chatbot that can answer questions about my skills, projects, and background directly within the portfolio. The bot is trained specifically on my professional content and provides concise, relevant responses.

Tech Stack

  • HTML5
  • Tailwind CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Chatbase AI platform

Key Takeaways

  • Improved accessibility of portfolio information
  • Hands-on experience embedding third-party AI tools
  • Consideration of UX tradeoffs when adding interactive features
